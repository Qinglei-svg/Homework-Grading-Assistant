Topic: My Favorite Book

My favorite book is The Little Prince. It is a short story, but it gives me many ideas about friendship and responsibility. The little prince travels to different planets and meets different people. Some of them only care about power or numbers, so they forget what is truly important.

I like this book because it tells us to keep a kind heart. The language is simple, but the meaning is deep. After reading it, I learned that we should care about the people and things we love.
